### Recent Achievements (2020-2025)

In the recent years, S.L. Benfica has continued to solidify its status as one of the most successful football clubs in Portugal. The club won its record-extending 38th Primeira Liga title in the 2022-23 season, clinching the championship on the final day of the season. This victory marked a significant milestone, reinforcing Benfica's dominance in Portuguese football. Additionally, Benfica claimed the Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira in 2023, adding to its impressive trophy cabinet.

### Current Performance (2023-2025)

As of the 2023-24 season, Benfica remains competitive in both domestic and European competitions. The team is currently positioned second in the Primeira Liga, showcasing a strong performance with 25 wins, 5 draws, and 4 losses. The squad has demonstrated a solid offensive and defensive record, scoring 77 goals while conceding only 28. In European competitions, Benfica has qualified for the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals for the second consecutive year, highlighting their resurgence on the continental stage.

### Latest Developments (2025)

Looking ahead to 2025, Benfica is poised for further growth and success. The club's management, led by President Rui Costa, is focused on maintaining competitive performance while also investing in player development through its youth academy, Benfica Campus. The club has also seen a resurgence in membership, with reports indicating a membership count of over 400,000, making it one of the largest sports clubs in the world by membership. This growth reflects the club's strong community support and commitment to its fans. As Benfica continues to build on its recent successes, the expectations for future achievements remain high.
