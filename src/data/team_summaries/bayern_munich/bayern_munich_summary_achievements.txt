# Bayern Munich: A Summary of Historical Achievements

## Major Trophies

Bayern Munich is the most successful club in German football history, boasting an impressive collection of trophies across domestic and international competitions:

### Domestic Achievements
- **Bundesliga Titles**: 34 (most recent in 2024-25)
- **DFB-Pokal (German Cup)**: 20 titles (most recent in 2019-20)
- **DFB/DFL-Supercup**: 10 titles (most recent in 2022)

### International Achievements
- **UEFA Champions League**: 6 titles (1974, 1975, 1976, 2001, 2013, 2020)
- **UEFA Cup/Europa League**: 1 title (1996)
- **UEFA Cup Winners' Cup**: 1 title (1967)
- **UEFA Super Cup**: 2 titles (2013, 2020)
- **FIFA Club World Cup**: 2 titles (2013, 2020)
- **Intercontinental Cup**: 2 titles (1976, 2001)

### Trebles
Bayern Munich has achieved the treble (winning the Bundesliga, DFB-Pokal, and UEFA Champions League in the same season) twice:
- **Seasonal Treble**: 2012-13, 2019-20
- **Domestic Treble**: 1999-2000

### Sextuple
In 2020, Bayern became the second club in history to win the sextuple, achieving six trophies in a single calendar year.

## Records & Milestones

- **Most Bundesliga Titles**: Bayern holds the record for the most Bundesliga championships, with 34 titles.
- **Consecutive Titles**: Bayern won the Bundesliga for 11 consecutive seasons from 2012-13 to 2022-23.
- **European Dominance**: Bayern is the only club to have won all three major European competitions (Champions League, UEFA Cup, and Cup Winners' Cup).
- **Three Consecutive European Cups**: Bayern won the European Cup three times in a row from 1974 to 1976.
- **Record Goal Scorer**: Robert Lewandowski set a Bundesliga record by scoring 41 goals in a single season (2020-21).

## Historical Significance

Bayern Munich's historical significance extends beyond its trophy cabinet. Founded in 1900, the club has grown to become a symbol of German football and a powerhouse in European competitions.

- **Cultural Impact**: Bayern has a massive global fanbase, being one of the most recognized football clubs worldwide. The club's motto, "Mia San Mia" (We are who we are), reflects its pride and identity.
- **Community Engagement**: Bayern has a long-standing commitment to social responsibility, contributing to various charitable causes and supporting struggling clubs financially.
