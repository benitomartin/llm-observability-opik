### Chelsea Football Club: Historical Achievements

#### 1. Major Trophies
Chelsea FC, founded in 1905, has a rich history of success both domestically and internationally. The club's major trophies include:

- **Domestic Competitions:**
  - **Premier League / First Division Titles:** 6 (1954–55, 2004–05, 2005–06, 2009–10, 2014–15, 2016–17)
  - **FA Cup:** 8 (1969–70, 1996–97, 1999–2000, 2006–07, 2008–09, 2009–10, 2011–12, 2017–18)
  - **Football League Cup / EFL Cup:** 5 (1964–65, 1997–98, 2004–05, 2006–07, 2014–15)
  - **FA Charity Shield / Community Shield:** 4 (1955, 2000, 2005, 2009)

- **International Competitions:**
  - **UEFA Champions League:** 2 (2011–12, 2020–21)
  - **UEFA Europa League:** 2 (2012–13, 2018–19)
  - **UEFA Cup Winners' Cup:** 2 (1970–71, 1997–98)
  - **UEFA Super Cup:** 2 (1998, 2021)
  - **FIFA Club World Cup:** 1 (2021)
  - **UEFA Conference League:** 1 (2024–25)

Chelsea is notably the first English club to win all three major UEFA trophies and the only club to hold the Champions League and Europa League titles simultaneously.

#### 2. Records & Milestones
Chelsea FC has achieved numerous records and milestones throughout its history:

- **Firsts:**
  - First club to play with shirt numbers in a match (1928).
  - First English side to travel by aeroplane for a domestic match (1957).
  - First Premier League team to field an entirely foreign starting lineup (1999).

- **League Records:**
  - Fewest goals conceded in a league season (15 in 2004–05).
  - Most consecutive home league matches without defeat (86 from 2004 to 2008).
  - First Premier League team to score 100 goals in a single season (2009–10).

- **Financial Milestones:**
  - Chelsea broke the British transfer record three times, with the signing of Kepa Arrizabalaga for £71 million in 2018 being the highest fee for a goalkeeper.

#### 3. Historical Significance
Chelsea FC has played a pivotal role in the evolution of English football:

- **Cultural Impact:** The club has a significant global following, with a strong presence in social media and merchandise sales. Chelsea was ranked among the top clubs worldwide in terms of brand value and commercial revenue.

- **Modern Era Success:** The club's transformation began in the early 2000s under Roman Abramovich's ownership, leading to unprecedented success and financial stability. This era saw Chelsea become a dominant force in both English and European football.

- **Community Engagement:** Chelsea has been involved in various community initiatives, including support for the National Health Service during the COVID-19 pandemic, showcasing the club's commitment to social responsibility.

Chelsea FC's storied history, marked by significant achievements and milestones, continues to shape its identity as one of the most successful and influential football clubs in the world.
