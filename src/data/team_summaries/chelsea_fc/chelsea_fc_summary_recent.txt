### Recent Achievements (2020-2025)

Chelsea Football Club has continued to build on its rich history with significant achievements in recent years. Notably, the club secured its second UEFA Champions League title in 2021, defeating Manchester City 1-0 in the final, thanks to a goal from Kai Havertz. Following this success, Chelsea also won the UEFA Super Cup in August 2021, triumphing over Villarreal in a penalty shootout after a 1-1 draw. In February 2022, the club made history by winning its first FIFA Club World Cup, beating Brazilian side Palmeiras 2-1 in the final.

In the 2024-2025 season, Chelsea added to its trophy cabinet by winning the UEFA Conference League, defeating Real Betis 4-1 in the final, thus becoming the first team to win all major European trophies.

### Current Performance (2023-2025)

The club has seen a mixed performance in the Premier League and domestic competitions during the 2023-2024 season. After a challenging start, Chelsea appointed Mauricio Pochettino as manager, who led the team to a sixth-place finish, securing a spot in the Conference League play-off round qualification. The team also reached the EFL Cup final in 2024 but narrowly lost to Liverpool 1-0.
